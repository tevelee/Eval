  • TemplateVariable

    A special kind of variable, that is used in case of TemplateInterpreters. It does not convert its content using the interpreterForEvaluatingVariables, but always uses the TemplateInterpreter instance. It’s perfect for expressions, that have a body, that needs to be further interpreted, such as an if or while statement.
